Delete Your Account & Data

Calp AI — AI calorie & nutrition tracker

This page explains how to request deletion of your Calp AI account and the personal data associated with it. You can delete your account directly inside the app at any time.

Option 1 — Delete in the app (recommended)

  1. Open Calp AI on your device.
  2. Go to the Profile tab.
  3. Scroll down and tap Delete account.
  4. Confirm. Your account and associated data are permanently removed.

Option 2 — Request deletion by email

If you can no longer access the app, send a deletion request to slezul99@gmail.com from the email address linked to your account. Include the subject line "Delete my Calp AI account". We will process the request within 30 days.
